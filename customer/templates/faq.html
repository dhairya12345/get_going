Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GET GOING ride-sharing platform

General Questions

What is GET GOING?

GET GOING is a ride-sharing platform that connects drivers with empty seats to passengers traveling in the same direction. It helps drivers earn money by sharing their rides and provides passengers with affordable transportation options.

How does GET GOING work?

Drivers can post their upcoming trips with available seats, specifying the route, date, time, and price. Passengers can search for rides matching their travel plans and book seats directly through the platform.

Is GET GOING available in my city?

GET GOING is currently available in most major cities across India. You can check availability by entering your location in the search bar on our homepage.

How much does it cost to use GET GOING?

Creating an account and browsing rides is completely free. Drivers pay a small service fee (10-15%) only when they successfully complete a ride with passengers. Passengers pay the price set by the driver for their seat.

For Drivers

How do I become a driver on GET GOING?

To become a driver, simply sign up for an account, verify your identity and driver's license, and add your vehicle details. Once approved, you can start posting your rides.

How much can I earn as a driver?

Earnings vary based on how often you drive, the distance of your trips, and the number of passengers you carry. Many drivers earn enough to cover their fuel costs and maintenance expenses.

Can I set my own prices?

Yes, you have full control over your pricing. You can set a price per seat or for the entire ride. Our system will suggest competitive prices based on the route and demand.

What if passengers cancel at the last minute?

If a passenger cancels less than 24 hours before the trip, they will be charged a cancellation fee which will be credited to your account.

For Riders

How do I book a ride?

Search for rides matching your route and travel date, select a suitable option, and click "Book Now." You'll need to make payment to confirm your booking.

How much does a ride cost?

Prices are set by drivers and are typically 30-50% cheaper than traditional taxis or cabs. The exact price is displayed before you book.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before the trip for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.

How do I contact my driver?

Once your booking is confirmed, you can message the driver directly through our secure messaging system to coordinate pickup details.

Safety

Is GET GOING safe?

Safety is our top priority. All drivers undergo identity verification and license checks. We also have user ratings, trip tracking, and emergency contact features.

What safety features does GET GOING offer?

We offer profile verification, real-time ride tracking, emergency assistance, and a robust rating system to ensure safe experiences for everyone.

What should I do if I feel unsafe during a ride?

You can use the emergency button in the app to alert authorities, or end the ride at the nearest safe location. Please report any safety concerns to us immediately.

Payments

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.

When is payment deducted for riders?

Payment is collected when you book a ride. If the ride is canceled by the driver, you'll receive a full refund.

How do drivers receive their earnings?

Earnings are transferred to your registered bank account weekly. You can track all payments in your driver dashboard.

Our support team is ready to help you with any other questions you might have.
